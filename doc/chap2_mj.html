2 Operations

The generic wreath product elements and in particular their (sparse) wreath cycle decompositions can be used to speed up certain operations in wreath products.

2.1 Operations List

Here we include a list of operations that take advantage of the generic wreath product elements representation.

In the following \(G = K \wr H\) denotes a wreath product and \(x,y \in G\) are wreath product elements.

  • Order(x)

  • x in G

  • RepresentativeAction(G, x, y)

  • IsConjugate(G, x, y)

  • Centraliser(G, x)

  • ConjugacyClasses(G)
